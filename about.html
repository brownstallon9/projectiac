ABOUT US

Our Vision

  • To Impact – with Allah’s support- the knowledge, skills and positive attitude necessary for children to grow into life with Islamic virtues and excellence.
  • To empower children with intellectual and moral principles needed to excel in this life and the hereafter and be the role model of their generation.
  • To be Nigeria’s leading educational institution focused on and dedicated to producing well-rounded and proudly Nigerian young men and women equipped for continuous learning, personal mastery and leadership.

Our Mission

  • To provide first class international Primary and Post-Primary Education for the over-all development of the teeming Muslim children at an affordable cost. The Academy assures you of a unique standard and balance education based on the tarbiyah and tasfiyah model.

What we Offer?

At Independence Academy, we proudly bridge the realms of Islamic and Western education, fostering an inclusive environment that celebrates diversity. Our curriculum seamlessly integrates the wisdom of Islamic teachings with the rigors of Western academia. Students embark on a journey of holistic development, gaining not only academic prowess but also a deep understanding of their cultural heritage and global perspectives. With state-of-the-art facilities, dedicated faculty, and a commitment to character education, we provide an educational experience that harmonizes tradition with innovation. Join us at the crossroads of knowledge, where the best of both worlds converge for a well-rounded and enriching learning adventure.

Academic Excellence:

  • Rigorous and comprehensive curriculum designed to foster intellectual growth.
  • Diverse range of academic programs catering to individual learning styles.
  • Highly qualified and dedicated faculty committed to student success.

Extracurricular Enrichment:

  • Vibrant array of extracurricular activities, including sports, arts,and clubs.
  • Opportunities for students to explore and develop their talents beyond the classroom.
  • A supportive environment that encourages well-rounded personal development.

Advanced Learning Opportunities:

  • Offering Advanced Placement (AP) courses or International Baccalaureate (IB) programs.
  • Specialized courses to challenge and engage academically ambitious students.

Cutting-Edge Facilities:

  • State-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, and technology infrastructure.
  • Extensive sports facilities, encouraging physical fitness and teamwork.

College and Career Readiness:

  • Comprehensive college counseling services to guide students in their future endeavors.
  • Career development programs and internships to prepare students for post-graduation success.

Character Education:

  • Focus on character development, instilling values such as integrity, responsibility, and empathy.
  • Social and emotional learning programs to nurture well-rounded individuals.

Innovation in Teaching:

  • Innovative teaching methods, including project-based learning and hands-on experiences.
  • Incorporation of technology to enhance the learning experience.

Parental Involvement:

  • Opportunities for parents to actively participate in their child's education.
  • Parent-teacher associations, workshops, and regular communication channels.

Our Instructors

At Independence Academy, our instructors are the heart of our educational journey. Passionate, experienced, and dedicated, they bring subject expertise and a commitment to nurturing each student's potential. Beyond imparting knowledge, our instructors serve as mentors, guiding students through their academic and personal development. With a diverse and talented team, we ensure a dynamic learning environment that fosters curiosity and a love for learning. At Independence Academy, our instructors inspire, guide, and empower students to excel academically and become confident, compassionate individuals ready to face the future with resilience and enthusiasm.

The school is committed to the training of skilled and innovative work-force that would transform Nigeria’s natural resources into goods and services driven by science and Information and Communication Technology (ICT), to positively affect the economy and thus the quality of life of her people.
